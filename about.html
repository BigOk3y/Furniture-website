Our Story

Started in a garage.
Built on craftsmanship.

Oakhaven was born in 2018 when our founder, Omar Al-Kuwari, watched a beautiful walnut dining table he'd bought new start to wobble and split within eighteen months. Frustrated by an industry built on particleboard and glue, he picked up a set of hand tools and built a replacement himself — solid, simple, and made to last.

What began as furniture built for friends and neighbours quickly grew into a small workshop, then a proper studio. Today, Oakhaven furnishes homes across Qatar and the Gulf, and every piece still starts with the same question: "Would this still be standing in twenty years?"

We're part of a community of homeowners who believe furniture should be an investment, not a disposable purchase — and that solid wood shouldn't cost a fortune.

What We Stand For

Our Values

🌳

Solid Wood Only

Every piece starts with real, named timber — oak, walnut, beech. No particleboard, no MDF, no mystery composites.

🪚

Traditional Joinery

Our frames are built with mortise-and-tenon joints by hand, tested for durability before anything leaves the workshop.

🧵

Honest Materials

We believe you should know exactly what's inside every cushion and frame. If it wouldn't last, it's not in our furniture.

🤝

Honest Business

No hidden delivery fees, no hard sells. Just great furniture, transparent pricing, and a team that answers the phone.

📦

Delivered & Assembled

Built in small batches and delivered by our own team, who assemble everything on-site so it's ready to use.

♻️

Responsibly Sourced

Our timber comes from managed forests, and offcuts are repurposed into smaller pieces rather than thrown away.
